Use the drink dispenser

When in space it’s important to stay hydrated but at the same time it can be difficult to drink fluids without having liquid floating around.
  1. Launch your personal Primary Control dialog.
  2. Select Drinks.
    ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:
    If the tube is not at your usual seat, and you are using someone else’s dispenser, first change the straw tip.
    1. Remove the numbered and color coded straw tip that is currently in use.
    2. Replace the straw tip with another one from the Straw Tip Repository located next to the straw.
    STEP RESULT:
    A seat slot opens exposing the drinking tube.
  3. Place the drinking tube into your mouth.
  4. Tap Options.
  5. Select the type of drink you wish to consume.
    ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:
    If you want a drink such as an Earl Grey tea with a bit of honey in it, then select Hot. If you want a room temperature drink, select Cool. For an icy beverage, select Cold.
    • Hot includes drinks served noticeably above room temperature.
    • Cool includes drinks served at or close to room temperature.
    • Cold includes drinks served well below room temperature.
  6. Choose the specific drink you desire.
    ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:
    Based on your choice you may have to make further selections.
AFTER COMPLETING THE TASK
Suck on the drinking tube and enjoy the beverage of your choice.
