Verifying a fire alarm

In this topic, you learn how to verify a fire alarm issued by a smoke detector.
PREREQUISITE
  • You have enabled administrator options.
  • You have the "Notify - smoke" user right assigned to you.
  1. Open the Smart Security application.
  2. Navigate to Activity logsAlarmsActive alarms.
    STEP RESULT: You see a list of all the active alarms. In case you received a message just for one fire alarm, this should be the only entry in the list.
    Note: If there is more than one entry in Active alarms, check the other alarms as well.
  3. Click on the entry with the active fire alarm.
    STEP RESULT: You have several options:
    • Directly alarm users
    • Check live view
    • Check virtual map
  4. If you are sure about the alarm, click Send alarm message.
    STEP RESULT: In addition to the alarm tone in the building, all users and administrators with the "Notify - smoke (on-premise)" user right receive a message to their registered devices.
    Important: If nobody verifies the alarm, the message is sent automatically after a short period of time.
  5. Before sending an alarm message, you can check a live view from the building. Below the list of active alarms, click Check live view.
    STEP RESULT: You receive a list of all the cameras and their positions.
  6. Select the camera in the area of the active fire alarm.
  7. Verify the fire alarm.
    • If the fire alarm is correct, click Confirm alarm.
    • If the fire alarm is false, click Dismiss alarm.
